Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Who We Are

Antu Technology ("we," "us," or "our") operates an email-marketing platform for Taiwan's education sector. Our registered email address is admin@antu-technology.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to communications from us or our customers, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Email engagement data (opens, clicks, unsubscribes)
  • Device and browser type (from email client headers)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Send email campaigns you have subscribed to
  • Measure campaign performance and improve deliverability
  • Honor unsubscribe requests promptly

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. AMP for Email

Some emails we send use AMP for Email technology, which enables interactive content within supported mail clients (Gmail, Yahoo Mail, Mail.ru). AMP emails may make server-side requests to load dynamic content. No additional personal data is collected beyond standard email engagement signals.

5. Data Retention

We retain subscriber data for as long as you remain subscribed. Upon unsubscribe, your data is removed within 30 days except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at admin@antu-technology.com. All emails include an unsubscribe link.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard measures including TLS encryption for email transmission, SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication on our sending domain.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy: admin@antu-technology.com
