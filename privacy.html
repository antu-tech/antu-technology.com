Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Who We Are

Antu Technology ("we," "us," or "our") builds email marketing infrastructure for businesses. We are reachable at info@antu-technology.com.

Information We Collect

When you or your subscribers interact with services we operate, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Email engagement signals (opens, clicks, unsubscribes)
  • Device and mail client type (from standard email headers)

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Deliver email campaigns subscribers have opted into
  • Measure deliverability and campaign performance
  • Honor unsubscribe and deletion requests promptly

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for their own marketing.

AMP for Email

Some messages we send use AMP for Email, an open standard that enables interactive content inside supported mail clients (Gmail, Yahoo Mail, Mail.ru). AMP emails may make server-side requests to render dynamic content. No additional personal data is collected beyond standard engagement signals.

Data Retention

We retain subscriber data for as long as the subscription is active. Upon unsubscribe, data is removed within 30 days except where retention is required by law.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting info@antu-technology.com. Every email we send includes a one-click unsubscribe link.

Security

We use TLS encryption for all email transmission, and authenticate our sending domain with SPF, DKIM, and DMARC.
